Philosopher's Walk

This app combines walking with philosophical contemplation. It's designed to be used while you walk — outside, around your neighborhood, or even pacing indoors.

1
Choose a duration (5-30 minutes) and a question to contemplate.
2
Start walking. The app will show timed contemplation prompts that deepen your inquiry — from opening reflection to practical application.
3
Don't rush to answer. Let the question walk with you. The prompts change automatically at intervals throughout your walk.
4
When the timer ends, you'll see a completion screen. The question continues to work in you after the walk.

Best experienced outdoors with headphones off — let the world be your soundtrack.
